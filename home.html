Why SpaceX matters

Founded by Elon Musk in 2002, SpaceX has transformed from a scrappy startup into the world's most valuable private space company.

Starship & Starlink

The fully reusable Starship is set to make Mars colonization feasible. Starlink already serves 5M+ subscribers across 100+ countries.

NASA's primary partner

SpaceX is NASA's top contractor for crew and cargo missions to the ISS — with the Crew Dragon and Falcon 9 setting the gold standard for reusability.

Explosive growth

Valuation grew from $74B in 2021 to over $350B in 2024 — making it one of the most valuable private companies in history.
